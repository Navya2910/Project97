Secondary School Education

In class 11th selecting the Humanities or Arts stream comprehends law-related subjects better, and it will be a fundamental starting point for your law course. In Humanities, you will learn subjects like Economics, Psychology, Geography, Sociology, Philosophy, Human Rights, and Gender Studies.



Qualifications Needed

Students must pass 10+2 with a minimum 60% aggregate score or equivalent CGPA

Students must clear entrance exams like CLAT, LSAT, AILET

Aspirants must complete bachelor's or integrated course in law

Candidates must clear AIBE exam to practice law
